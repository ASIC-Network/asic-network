WHAT IT IS

The ASIC Network is a collective of university ASIC and VLSI clubs. Independent student groups, working in the open, sharing what we learn.

Chip design is hard to break into and easy to do alone. The network pools that effort. Right now that means shared documentation and a biweekly cadence where clubs trade progress, flows, and gotchas. No gatekeeping, no silos, just student groups building together.
